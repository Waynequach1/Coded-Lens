The Code

One of the greatest misconception about technology is that technology cannot discriminate. In paritcular, we base it off the following misconception:

"Physics is physics."

No matter what reason, physics will continue to do what it does. An apple will not stop falling from a tree even if the entire world is on fire. And so We say that "Technology is technology." and apply the same rationality. Which leads us towards the conclusion that technology is unbiased.

But this thinking is DANGEROUS

Hidden behind every technology is a developer, a human. 
            a developer with skills, talents, and capabilities. a human
                        a developer with a favorite lunch, favorite snack, favorite person. a human
                                    a developer with who hates working on mondays and the traffic on the way home. a human
                                                a developer with BIASES. And yet a human

Every technology is thus built by developers with biases and every piece of technology flawed with personal bias.

Equip the lens to explore the various ways technology has been affected by these biases.
